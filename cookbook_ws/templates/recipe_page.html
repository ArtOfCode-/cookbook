Creamy Mashed Sweet Potatoes

Source: Cook's Country December/January 2007

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into 4 pieces
  • 3 tablespoons heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 pounds sweet potatoes (2 large or 3 medium), peeled, quartered lengthwise and cut into 1/4 inch slices
  • salt and pepper
Note

It is imperative to cut the sweet potato into thin, even slices to ensure perfect cooking.

  1. Quarter each peeled sweet potato lengthwise.
  2. Cut each quarter into 1/2-inch slices crosswise.

Instructions

  1. Combine butter, 2 tablespoons cream, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon pepper, sugar, and sweet potatoes in a large saucepan.
  2. Cook, covered, over low heat untll potatoes are fall-aparet tender, 35 to 40 minutes.
  3. Off head, add remaining tablespoon cream and mash sweet potatoes with potato masher.
  4. Serve

SERVES 4 TO 6
